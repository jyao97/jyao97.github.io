Hello 👋, I'm Jianpeng Yao, a first-year Ph.D. student at <a href='https://tasl.ucr.edu/'>TASL</a>, advised by <a href='https://jiachenli94.github.io/'>Prof. Jiachen Li</a>. My research interests lie in robotics and machine learning (especially in reinforcement learning), with a focus on developing trustworthy algorithms for real-world robotic applications.

Before attending UCR, I earned my Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Electrical Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), China. I conducted research on introducing reinforcement learning to magnetic soft robots when I was a graduate student at HUST and then worked as an algorithm engineer in the autonomous driving industry for a year.
